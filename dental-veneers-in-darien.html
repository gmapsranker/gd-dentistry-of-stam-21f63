Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Veneers

How much do dental veneers cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
At our Darien office, porcelain veneers typically range from $1,500 to $2,500 per tooth, depending on the complexity of your case and the type of veneer selected. We offer both traditional porcelain and minimal-prep veneers. Most patients get 6-8 veneers for their front teeth, bringing the total investment to $9,000-$20,000. We provide free consultations and offer CareCredit financing options with monthly payment plans to help make the treatment more affordable.
How long does the entire veneer process take from consultation to final placement?
The complete veneer process at our Darien location typically takes 2-3 weeks. Your initial consultation and treatment planning takes about 1 hour. During your first treatment appointment (about 2 hours), we prepare your teeth and take impressions for your custom veneers. You'll wear temporary veneers for 1-2 weeks while our local dental lab crafts your permanent ones. The final placement appointment takes approximately 2 hours, during which we ensure perfect fit and comfort before permanent bonding.
Do you offer any warranty or guarantee on your dental veneers?
Yes, we stand behind our work with a 5-year warranty on all porcelain veneers placed at GD Dentistry of Stamford. This covers any defects in materials or workmanship. If a veneer chips, cracks, or debonds during normal use within this period, we'll replace it at no cost to you. The warranty requires maintaining regular 6-month checkups at our office and following our care instructions. We also provide a temporary warranty during the adjustment period to ensure you're completely satisfied with the fit and appearance.
Will my insurance cover any portion of the veneer procedure?
Most dental insurance plans in Connecticut consider veneers a cosmetic procedure and typically don't cover the cost. However, if your veneers are needed due to dental trauma or severe tooth damage, insurance may cover a portion. Our office staff will verify your benefits and can submit a pre-treatment estimate to your insurance provider. We also offer an in-house dental savings plan and partner with CareCredit to provide 6-12 month interest-free financing options.
How long do veneers typically last, and what maintenance is required?
With proper care, veneers placed at our Darien office typically last 10-15 years. To maintain them, we recommend: brushing twice daily with a non-abrasive toothpaste, flossing daily, wearing a night guard if you grind your teeth (which we can provide), and scheduling regular 6-month checkups at our office. Avoid biting into very hard foods with your veneers and using your teeth as tools. We'll provide detailed care instructions and include free veneer maintenance checks during your regular dental cleanings.
