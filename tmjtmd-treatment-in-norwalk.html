Frequently Asked Questions About TMJ/TMD Treatment

How much does TMJ treatment typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford?

Basic treatment plans involving custom nightguards and exercises typically range from $800-$1,500. More complex treatments involving dental work or Botox therapy can range from $2,000-$4,000. We accept most major insurance plans and offer CareCredit financing options to help make treatment more affordable. We'll provide a detailed cost breakdown during your consultation.
What types of TMJ treatment methods do you offer?
At GD Dentistry, we offer multiple treatment approaches based on your specific condition. Our primary treatments include custom-fitted orthotic devices and nightguards, therapeutic Botox injections to relax overactive jaw muscles, physical therapy exercises, and dental treatments to correct bite issues. For severe cases, we also provide occlusal adjustments and can coordinate with local specialists in Fairfield County if surgical intervention is needed. Dr. Gordon Davidson personally evaluates each patient to determine the most effective treatment combination.
How long does it typically take to see improvement in TMJ symptoms?
Most patients begin experiencing relief within 2-4 weeks of starting treatment. However, complete improvement can take 3-6 months depending on your condition's severity and compliance with the treatment plan. We schedule follow-up appointments every 2-3 weeks initially to monitor your progress and adjust treatment as needed. Some patients may require ongoing management to prevent symptom recurrence. We've found that patients who consistently follow their prescribed treatment plan and exercises typically see the fastest improvement.
