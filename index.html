How long does the Invisalign process take at your Greenwich office?
Most of our Invisalign patients complete their treatment in 12-18 months. However, the exact timeline varies based on your specific needs. Minor alignment issues may take as little as 6 months, while more complex cases could take up to 24 months. We'll provide a precise timeline during your consultation. Your initial consultation at GD Dentistry takes about 60 minutes. We'll also discuss costs, insurance coverage, and payment options. This consultation is complimentary and includes a take-home simulation of your projected results.
