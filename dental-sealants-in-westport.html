Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Sealants

How much do dental sealants cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
At GD Dentistry of Stamford, dental sealants typically cost between $35-$60 per tooth. Many dental insurance plans in Connecticut cover sealants for children under 18, often at 100%. For patients without insurance, we offer flexible payment plans and accept CareCredit. We recommend calling our office with your specific insurance information for an accurate cost estimate.
How long does the dental sealant procedure take?
The entire sealant procedure at our Westport office usually takes 20-30 minutes for 2-4 teeth. Each individual tooth takes about 5-10 minutes. The process includes cleaning the tooth, applying an etching solution, rinsing, drying, and applying the sealant material. The sealant hardens quickly under our special curing light, so you can eat and drink normally right after leaving our office.
How long do dental sealants last?
With proper care, dental sealants from GD Dentistry typically last 5-10 years. We warranty our sealants for 2 years - if any sealant falls out or fails during this period, we'll replace it at no cost. During your regular 6-month checkups at our Westport location, we'll examine your sealants and let you know if any need to be replaced.
At what age should my child get dental sealants?
We typically recommend dental sealants as soon as the permanent molars emerge, usually between ages 6-12. For most children in Westport, the first molars appear around age 6, with second molars coming in around age 12. Early application helps prevent decay during these cavity-prone years. We'll evaluate your child's specific needs during their examination to determine the optimal timing.
Does getting dental sealants hurt? Do you need anesthesia?
The dental sealant procedure is completely painless and requires no anesthesia or drilling. Our experienced team at GD Dentistry simply cleans the tooth, applies the liquid sealant, and hardens it with a special light. The process is quick and comfortable, making it ideal even for young children or anxious patients. We take extra care to ensure our Westport patients are comfortable throughout the procedure.
