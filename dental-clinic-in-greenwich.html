Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Clinic

Do you accept dental insurance? Which plans do you work with?
Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and United Healthcare. We're also in-network with many Connecticut-based insurance providers. Before your appointment, we'll verify your coverage and provide a detailed breakdown of any out-of-pocket costs. If you're unsure about coverage, our office staff can help check your benefits - just call us at (203) 555-0123 with your insurance information.
How long do I need to wait for an appointment? Do you handle dental emergencies?
For routine cleanings and check-ups, we typically schedule 2-3 weeks out. However, we reserve daily slots for dental emergencies and can usually see emergency cases same-day or next-day. If you're experiencing severe tooth pain, broken teeth, or other urgent dental issues, call us immediately - we prioritize emergency cases for our Greenwich and Stamford area patients. For non-emergency new patients, your first appointment will be scheduled within 1-2 weeks.
What's the cost for a dental crown at your office?
At GD Dentistry, porcelain crowns typically range from $900-$1,500 per tooth, depending on the specific material and complexity. With insurance, patients usually pay between $300-$600 out-of-pocket. We offer free consultations to provide exact pricing for your specific case. All our crowns are crafted in our partner lab in Connecticut and come with a 5-year warranty. We also offer payment plans through CareCredit to help make treatment more affordable.
Do you offer sedation dentistry for anxious patients?
Yes, we provide several sedation options for anxious patients. We offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) for mild anxiety ($75 additional fee), and oral conscious sedation for moderate anxiety ($150-200). Dr. Garcia is certified by the State of Connecticut in sedation dentistry and has over 15 years of experience helping anxious patients. During your consultation, we'll discuss your anxiety level and recommend the most appropriate sedation method.
Where is your office located and what are your hours?
Our office is located at 123 Main Street in Greenwich, CT, just off I-95 exit 3. We're in the medical building next to Greenwich Hospital with free parking available. We're open Monday-Thursday 8am-6pm, Friday 8am-4pm, and two Saturdays per month 8am-2pm. We serve patients from Greenwich, Stamford, and surrounding Fairfield County communities.
