Frequently Asked Questions About Sleep Apnea Oral Appliances

Does insurance cover oral appliances for sleep apnea at your New Canaan office?
Most medical insurance plans (not dental insurance) cover oral appliances for sleep apnea when deemed medically necessary. We work with major insurance providers in Connecticut and typically see coverage ranging from 50-80% after deductible. Our office staff will verify your benefits and provide a detailed cost breakdown before treatment begins. We also offer flexible payment plans for any out-of-pocket expenses.
How long does it take to get a sleep apnea oral appliance from start to finish?
The complete process typically takes 2-3 weeks at our Stamford location. This includes: Initial consultation (1 hour), digital impressions of your teeth (30 minutes), custom appliance fabrication (7-10 business days), and a fitting appointment (45 minutes). We then schedule a follow-up visit after 2 weeks to ensure proper fit and effectiveness. Additional adjustments are included at no charge for the first 90 days.
What type of oral appliances do you offer and how do I know which is right for me?
At GD Dentistry, we primarily work with three FDA-approved devices: SomnoDent, TAP, and ProSomnus. During your consultation, Dr. Gardner will evaluate factors like your jaw structure, severity of apnea, and lifestyle needs to recommend the most suitable option. We've found the ProSomnus device works particularly well for side sleepers, while the TAP system is excellent for those who grind their teeth.
Do I need a sleep study before getting an oral appliance from your office?
Yes, a recent sleep study (within the last year) is required before we can fit you for an oral appliance. If you haven't had one, we work closely with several sleep centers in Fairfield County and can provide referrals. We accept both traditional in-lab and home sleep study results. Once we have your sleep study results, we can proceed with the appliance fitting process.
What kind of guarantee do you offer on your sleep apnea appliances?
We provide a 1-year warranty on all our sleep apnea oral appliances, covering manufacturing defects and fit issues. Additionally, we guarantee a minimum 50% reduction in your AHI (Apnea-Hypopnea Index) score, verified through a follow-up sleep study, or we'll refund the appliance cost. We also include 90 days of free adjustments to ensure optimal comfort and effectiveness.
