Frequently Asked Questions About Same-Day Crowns

How much do same-day crowns typically cost at GD Dentistry, and do you accept insurance?
At GD Dentistry of Stamford, same-day crowns typically range from $800-$1,500 depending on the specific tooth and complexity. We accept most major dental insurance plans, including Delta Dental, Cigna, and Aetna, which usually cover 50-70% of crown procedures. We also offer CareCredit financing and in-house payment plans. We're happy to verify your insurance coverage and provide a detailed cost breakdown before beginning treatment.
How long does the entire same-day crown process take?
The entire same-day crown process at our Wilton office takes approximately 2-3 hours from start to finish. This includes the initial exam, preparing the tooth, taking digital impressions with our CEREC machine, designing your crown, milling it on-site, and final placement. Unlike traditional crowns that require 2-3 weeks and multiple visits, you'll leave our office with your permanent crown the same day. We recommend scheduling morning appointments to ensure completion within regular office hours.
What type of technology do you use for same-day crowns, and how durable are they?
We use the latest CEREC 3D technology at our Wilton practice to create precise, custom-fitted crowns. These crowns are milled from high-grade ceramic materials that match your natural tooth color and can last 10-15 years with proper care. Our ceramic crowns are just as durable as traditional lab-made crowns and are backed by our 5-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Dr. Gardner has over 15 years of experience with CEREC technology and has completed over 2,000 same-day crown procedures.
Is there any recovery time needed after getting a same-day crown?
There's minimal recovery time needed after getting a same-day crown. You can return to work or normal activities immediately after the procedure. Your tooth may feel slightly sensitive to hot and cold for a few days, which is normal. We recommend avoiding very hard or sticky foods for the first 24 hours while the cement fully sets. Most patients adjust to their new crown within a week. If you experience any unusual discomfort, we provide same-day adjustments at no additional cost.
What happens if my same-day crown breaks or doesn't fit correctly?
If there are any issues with your crown's fit or comfort, we'll address them immediately since we make the crowns in-house. Our 5-year warranty covers adjustments, repairs, or replacement if needed. In the rare case of a break or poor fit, we can usually mill a new crown the same day at no additional cost to you. We also provide emergency dental services to our Wilton and Stamford area patients if you experience any issues after hours. Our goal is to ensure you're completely satisfied with your crown's fit, function, and appearance.
